Privacy Policy

Last Updated: September 26, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how our app handles data and permissions when automating and publishing content to TikTok through the TikTok Developer API.

What Our App Does

Our app automates the publishing of short-form educational videos to TikTok. All content is created or approved by the account owner in advance.

What Data We Access

Through TikTok’s official API, our app may access:

  • Video Files & Captions: Provided by the account owner for posting.
  • Account Information: Basic profile and permissions required to connect the TikTok account.
  • Engagement Data (optional): Public metrics such as views, likes, or comments, to help evaluate performance.

We do not collect or access any personal data from TikTok users.

How We Use the Data

  • To upload and publish videos on behalf of the authenticated TikTok account.
  • To retrieve post IDs and engagement metrics to improve content strategy.
  • To maintain authentication tokens for posting (not shared or resold).

Data Storage and Sharing

  • We do not sell, share, or transfer TikTok data to third parties.
  • Data is processed only for the purpose of publishing content and analyzing performance.
  • We do not store personally identifiable information (PII) from TikTok users.
